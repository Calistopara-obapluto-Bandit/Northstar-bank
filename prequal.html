Home Loan Prequalification

Understand how much you can borrow and what to expect during the home lending process. Our prequalification tool evaluates your financial profile to give you a clear picture of your borrowing potential.

How Prequalification Works

Prequalification considers key financial factors including your income, savings, credit history, and existing debts. This preliminary assessment helps you understand your borrowing capacity and prepares you for the formal loan application process.

1

Income Verification

We review your income stability and employment history.

2

Credit Assessment

Your credit score and payment history are evaluated.

3

Debt Analysis

We assess your existing debts and obligations.

4

Estimate Provided

You receive a borrowing range estimate.

Next Steps

Once you're prequalified, our team will guide you through the formal application process. We're here to answer questions and help you move forward with confidence toward your home financing goals.

Complete Formal Application

Provide detailed financial documentation and complete the full loan application.

Home Appraisal & Inspection

We arrange for property appraisal and inspection to verify the home's value.

Final Approval

Upon completion of underwriting, you receive final loan approval.

Closing & Funding

Complete closing documents and receive your funds to finalize your purchase.
