Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account?

You can open an account online through our website in just a few minutes, or visit any of our branches to speak with a team member.

What are your current interest rates?

Visit our website or call us for the most up-to-date rates on all account types. Rates are competitive and updated regularly.

Is my money safe with NorthStar Bank?

Yes. Your deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000 per account category, and we use industry-leading security measures to protect your information.

How do I report fraudulent activity?

Contact our fraud department immediately at 1-800-NORTHSTAR or through our secure online platform. We monitor all accounts 24/7 for suspicious activity.
